Brief Bio

Shane Vincent Sedlemyer was born and raised in Western Pennsylvania. He holds a BA in integrative Arts (Theatre/Dance/Music) from Penn State. He has experience in several different disciplines of theatre (see credits below). He will receive his MA in English Literature from Indiana University of Pennsylvania in May 2017. In addition to his dramatic focus, Shane is an avid lover of film, video games, and comics.
